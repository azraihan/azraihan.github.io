I'm a final-year Computer Science and Engineering student at [Bangladesh University of Engineering and Technology (BUET)](https://www.buet.ac.bd/), working at the intersection of **multimodal AI**, **natural language processing**, and **AI systems research**.

My work explores how AI systems reason across languages and modalities — and how to make them more robust, efficient, and principled. I've used causal activation patching to localize the English-pivot bottleneck in LLaVA-1.5-7B and shown that training-free steering vectors at those layers improve multilingual VQA by up to 6.5 pp. I've designed **AdaGate**, a lightweight modality gate that cuts expensive vision-encoder calls by ~53% in streaming multimodal QA without sacrificing accuracy. I've probed how video SSL encoders (V-JEPA) crystallize affordance signal 4 layers earlier than image SSL encoders, enabling 5.3× attention compute reduction. And I've proposed **SpatialCoT**, a five-step geometry-grounded chain-of-thought that reduces viewpoint-rotation accuracy degradation in 3D-LLMs from 77% to 41%.

A thread running through all of this is an interest in the **systems layer** of AI: how multi-component inference pipelines can be made efficient, interpretable, and principled — rather than assemblies of hand-crafted prompts. This leads me toward questions about retrieval-augmented generation, prompt optimization, and declarative AI programming frameworks that treat LLM pipelines as first-class objects to be compiled and improved.

Beyond research, I've competed internationally and nationally with strong results. Our team **NeuronX** won **1st place at [IEEE VIP Cup 2025](https://signalprocessingsociety.org/community-involvement/video-image-processing-cup)** — beating 40+ international teams in multimodal RGB-IR fusion for drone detection, tracking, and payload classification, presented at ICIP 2025 in Anchorage, AK. Our team **BUET XFACTOR** took **1st place at the Poridhi.io × Brain Station 23 AI Engineering Hackathon** (86 teams, 300+ participants) building an intent-based multilingual product search system powered by in-memory LLM inference via `llama.cpp` — no GPU, half the allocated hardware. I also co-developed a low-resource ASR system for 20 regional Bangladeshi dialects, earning **1st Runner-Up** at the শব্দতরী datathon (CUET ETE Televerse 1.0).

I'm actively looking for PhD positions starting **Fall 2027**, with research interests spanning NLP, information retrieval, multimodal systems, and principled AI programming.
